About This Program

Life Saver was founded to reduce the critical shortage of safe blood in Bangladesh. We connect willing donors with hospitals and families facing emergencies. If you are healthy and aged 18–65, registering takes just two minutes. If you or a loved one urgently needs blood, submit a request below and our team will reach out within the hour.
